Crescent Moon Wall Art

Crescent Moon Shelf

After a refresher course in geometry, the design was simple. 3D modeling was all but necessary for ensure the pieces would properly fit together. Thanks to a few hours in Sketchup I was able to generate a cutlist.
Assembly of the overlapping dodecagons proved to be a monster of it's own. A roll of masking tape, and half a bottle of wood glue later; all it took was some distant perspective and scrupulous skewing to get the shape correct.


The mountains make a great bookshelf, and the rest of the inner shelving can hold knick knacks to the hearts desire.


If you find yourself with open wallspace, it makes a beautful addition to any room.

Floating Mantel
